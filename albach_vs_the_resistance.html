Mecha Design - “Albach vs The Resistance” (Personal Work)

Services: Illustration, VisDev

Character design ideas for two warring factions set in a post-apocalyptic cyberscape. The cybernetically enhanced ruling class faces resistance from the slum-raised children fighting to keep the last of their humanity.

Albach Cyborg

The “Albach” race, championing their progressive ideals, believe that the way forward as a species is to discard their human-hood and become one with the machine, converging towards a technological singularity, which they see as enlightenment.


Resistance Operator

A resistance group emerged to counter the Albach's ideals as they believe that the merging of man and machine will result in irreversible damage to human society and result in the extinction of the species.
